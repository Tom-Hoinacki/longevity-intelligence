# GLP-1 Research Notes

## Asset
GLP-1 Drugs

## What is proven?

- Significant weight loss in appropriate populations
- Strong evidence for diabetes treatment
- Strong evidence for reducing cardiovascular events in certain high-risk populations

## What is promising?

- Potential benefits for fatty liver disease
- Potential benefits for sleep apnea
- Potential benefits for inflammation-related outcomes

## What is speculative?

- General longevity extension
- Slowing biological aging
- Broad anti-aging effects in healthy individuals

## Questions to research

- Which trials are most important?
- Which biomarkers improve?
- What are the long-term muscle-loss concerns?
- What populations benefit most?
