# Evidence Scoring v1

Evidence Score:
0 = unsupported or misleading
1 = mechanism only
2 = cell or animal evidence
3 = observational or small human data
4 = good human trial data
5 = strong human outcome evidence

Hype Score:
0 = low hype
5 = claim far ahead of evidence

Risk Score:
0 = very low risk
5 = high risk, invasive, poorly regulated, or major unknowns
